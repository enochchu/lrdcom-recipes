Buttons

Button Colors

These are eight available colors for buttons, named by their respective colors to match the scheme of a given page.

Default button is the most prevalent type of buttons in primary color.

Complementary button is designed to highlight important actions such as download.

Dark button is intended for placement on banners with a light or pale background.

Light button is intended for placement on banners with a dark background.
